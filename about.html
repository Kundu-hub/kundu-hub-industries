Our Story

Founded in 1992, KUNDU INDUSTRIAL CORPORATION has established itself as a trusted provider of high-quality industrial plastics and rubber products, serving businesses across diverse industrial sectors.

With a strong B2B focus, we are dedicated to meeting the specialized requirements of pharmaceutical companies, industrial equipment suppliers, packaging manufacturers, and related industries.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction enables us to deliver products that support efficient operations and long-term business success. Backed by decades of industry experience, we strive to build lasting partnerships by providing dependable solutions tailored to the evolving needs of our clients.

Our Mission

To supply high-quality industrial plastics and rubber products with reliable service, helping businesses operate efficiently and grow with confidence.

Our Vision

To be a trusted B2B partner and leading supplier of industrial plastics and rubber products, known for quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Quality, Integrity, Reliability, and Customer Commitment are at the core of everything we do. We strive to deliver dependable industrial solutions while building lasting relationships based on trust and excellence.
